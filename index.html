Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our vehicle history reports

How reliable is a Car Verifireport compared to others in the market?
Car Verifireports are highly reliable, compiled from multiple trusted data sources including DMV records, insurance companies, and auto auctions. Our comprehensive reports cover all critical vehicle components, providing you with accurate and unbiased assessments you can trust.
How long does it take to get a vehicle history report?
Our reports are generated instantly after you enter a valid VIN and complete your purchase. You'll receive a comprehensive digital report within minutes, accessible through your account dashboard.
What information do I need to get a vehicle history report?
You only need the vehicle's 17-character VIN (Vehicle Identification Number). This can typically be found on the driver's side dashboard, door jamb, or vehicle registration documents.
Do you provide reports for vehicles outside the United States?
Yes, we provide vehicle history reports for cars from multiple countries including Canada, United Kingdom, Australia, Germany, France, and Japan. Select your country during the VIN check process.
Will I get a written vehicle inspection report?
Yes, you'll receive a comprehensive digital report with detailed findings, which can be downloaded, printed, or shared with sellers or mechanics. The report is accessible through our portal for future reference.
What if the report doesn't match the vehicle's actual condition?
While our reports are highly accurate based on available records, we recommend a physical inspection for complete assurance. If you find significant discrepancies in our report, contact our support team for review and potential refund.
Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied with the report?
We offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not satisfied with your report, contact us within 7 days of purchase for a full refund, provided you haven't used the report to complete a vehicle transaction.
