About Car Verifi

Your trusted partner in vehicle history reporting. We empower car buyers and sellers with comprehensive, accurate, and transparent vehicle data.

Our Story

Car Verifi has revolutionized the way people access vehicle history information.

Car Verifi was born from a simple observation: buying a used car shouldn't feel like a gamble. Our founder, after a disappointing experience with a used vehicle that had hidden issues, recognized the need for transparent, accessible vehicle history information.

What started as a small startup with a mission to provide honest vehicle reports has grown into a trusted platform serving thousands of customers across North America. We've partnered with major data providers, insurance companies, and government agencies to compile the most comprehensive vehicle history database available.

Today, we continue to innovate, developing new tools and features to make car buying and selling safer, smarter, and more transparent for everyone.

Our Mission & Vision

Driving transparency in the automotive industry through innovative technology and reliable data.
